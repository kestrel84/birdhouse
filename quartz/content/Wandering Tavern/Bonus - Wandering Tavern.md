Basic idea is to have the obelisk/engine/whatever be a giant heart at the center of a crystal mountain. The stuff the mountain is made of is destructible by a lot of sstuff but most importantly dynamite.

There's an old abandoned mine with a shop at the entrance which sells basic adventuring gear and 

stuff I can have in the crystal caverns:
- Explosives, both static and not
	- Glowing red crystals which explode if damaged (DC 13 3d6 15ft)
	- Dynamite (lots of this) (DC10 2d6 10ft)
	- Remote charges (DC10 2d6 20ft)
	- Proximity mines (DC10 2d6 10ft)
	- Fireworks (DC10 1d6 blindness 10ft)
	- explosive arrows (+1d6 5ft)
	- grenades (but they're like cartoon bombs) (DC10 2d6 10ft)
- Magic items (explosive or otherwise demolishing)
	- Wand of fireball
	- potion of makes your fists explode (works for excavation) (plus 1d6 fire damage on unarmed in 5ft radius)
	- instant wall
	- [sax-a-boom](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/wondrous-items:horn-of-blasting)
- Excavation items (not useful in combat)
	- Drills
	- Laser drills (can be used to cut stalactites for DC15 5d6 damage)
	- tunneller (implodes a long ass cylinder of terrain) (rare asf)
- Environmental effects
	- Massive resevoirs of oil in the wall which is leaking into the combat arenas and covering the floor
	- stalactites (lots, see above)
	- minecarts of dynamite
	- cannons
	- explosive cannons
	- gas leak
	- loot you don't want to blow up
